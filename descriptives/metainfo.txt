Name of Quantlet: SPL_descriptives

Published in: Statistical Programming Languages

Description: Quantlet consists of two parts. First: function desc that allows to visualize the distibution of 
dependent variable for many datasets within one folder. It accelarates the process of the paper replication.
Second: cdesc() - functionality check the usefullness of independent
variables, whether there are some variables with very low variability, or even constants, looks for the predictors that
are corelated to each other stronger than a given threshold. If these variables are present, their names will be printed out
and returned within the list, as well as descriptive summary and corelation table of all the predictors.

Keywords: descriptives, distribution, visualization, corelation, summary
